Retrieve the following supporting Bible verses for each of the nine fruits of the Holy Spirit:

- 1 Corinthians 13:4-7 (love)
- Nehemiah 8:10 and John 15:9-11 (joy)
- John 14:27 and Philippians 4:6-7 (peace)
- James 1:2-4 and Ephesians 4:2 (patience)
- Titus 3:4-5 and Ephesians 4:32 (kindness)
- Romans 3:10-12 and Matthew 5:16 (goodness)
- Lamentations 3:22-23 and Proverbs 17:17 (faithfulness)
- Matthew 11:28-30 and Philippians 4:5 (gentleness)
- 1 Corinthians 9:24-27 (self-control)

These verses will be used to enrich the teaching content for each lesson in the Youth Bible course on the fruits of the Holy Spirit.

- (`1Cor 13:4-7`) 4 Love is patient, love is kind, it is not envious. - (`John 14:27`) “Peace I leave with you; my peace I give to you; I do not give it to you as the world does. Do not let your hearts be distressed or lacking in courage.
- (`Phil 4:6-7`) 6 Do not be anxious about anything. Instead, in every situation, through prayer and petition with thanksgiving, tell your requests to God. 7 And the peace of God that surpasses all understanding will guard your hearts and minds in Christ Jesus.
- (`Jas 1:2-4`) 2 My brothers and sisters, consider it nothing but joy when you fall into all sorts of trials, 3 because you know that the testing of your faith produces endurance. 4 And let endurance have its perfect effect, so that you will be perfect and complete, not deficient in anything.
- (`Eph 4:2`) with all humility and gentleness, with patience, putting up with one another in love,
- (`Titus 3:4-5`) 4 But “when the kindness of God our Savior and his love for mankind appeared, 5 he saved us not by works of righteousness that we have done but on the basis of his mercy, through the washing of the new birth and the renewing of the Holy Spirit,
