# Title

Fruits of the Holy Spirit Youth Course

# Original Request



Prepare a comprehensive Youth Bible course (age-range: secondary school students; context: UK) about the fruits of the Holy Spirit. A total of 9 lessons, with each containing:

* ice-breakers

* detailed bible teaching with quoted bible verses

* illustrations, devotions and applications

* guidance for youth team helpers

* group discussion questions

# Refined Request

Create a comprehensive 9-lesson Youth Bible course for UK secondary school students (ages 11-16) exploring the fruits of the Holy Spirit (Galatians 5:22-23). Each lesson should be structured for a 60-minute session and include:

1. **Ice-breakers** - Two engagement activities per lesson (one quick warm-up, one deeper connection activity)

2. **Detailed Bible teaching** - Thorough exploration of each fruit with context, explanation, and direct Bible verse quotations (including surrounding passages for context)

3. **Illustrations, devotions, and applications** - Contemporary illustrations relevant to UK teenagers, personal reflection prompts, and practical ways to apply each fruit to daily life

4. **Guidance for youth team helpers** - Clear instructions for leaders, including potential discussion pitfalls, discipleship tips, and pastoral considerations for this age group

5. **Group discussion questions** - Open-ended questions suitable for mixed-ability secondary school students, with differentiation options for younger vs. older teens

The course should:
- Assign one fruit of the Spirit to each lesson (love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, self-control)
- Include a brief introductory lesson on the Holy Spirit and an overview of the fruits
- Feature age-appropriate content for UK teens, including culturally relevant examples
- Provide clear learning outcomes for each lesson
- Include optional extension activities for groups with more time
- Offer a concluding session that ties all nine fruits together and challenges students to live by the Spirit
