3. **Illustrations, Devotions, and Applications**: Provide relatable analogies, devotional content, and practical ways to apply the fruit in daily life.

5. **Group Discussion Questions**: List 3-5 open-ended questions to stimulate conversation and reflection.

Additionally, for the entire course:

- Recommend any necessary materials or resources.

Output the course as a well-organized document with distinct sections for each lesson and component.
