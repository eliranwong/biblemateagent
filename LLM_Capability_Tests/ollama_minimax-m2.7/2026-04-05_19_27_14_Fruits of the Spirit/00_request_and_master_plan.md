# Title

Fruits of the Spirit

---

# Original Request

---

Prepare a comprehensive Youth Bible course (age-range: secondary school students; context: UK) about the fruits of the Holy Spirit. A total of 9 lessons, with each containing:

* ice-breakers

* detailed bible teaching with quoted bible verses

* illustrations, devotions and applications

* guidance for youth team helpers

* group discussion questions

---

# Refined Request

---

Create a comprehensive 9-lesson Youth Bible course for UK secondary school students (Years 7-11, ages 11-16) exploring the Fruits of the Holy Spirit from Galatians 5:22-23 (love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control). Structure each lesson with the following consistent elements:

1. **Ice-breaker activity** – An engaging 10-minute opening activity suitable for mixed-ability groups
2. **Detailed Bible teaching** – A thorough teaching section with relevant Scripture passages fully quoted and explained, including cross-references where appropriate
3. **Illustrations, devotions, and applications** – Real-world examples, personal reflection prompts, and practical ways students can live out each fruit daily
4. **Guidance for youth team helpers** – Clear instructions and tips for leaders facilitating the session, including potential pitfalls and how to handle sensitive topics
5. **Group discussion questions** – Thought-provoking questions differentiated for deeper theological reflection

Additional requirements:
- Format each lesson as a complete, self-contained session lasting approximately 60-75 minutes
- Include a suggested lesson flow with approximate timings for each element
- Incorporate relevant UK cultural context and age-appropriate language
- Provide any necessary handouts or student worksheet templates
- Include optional extension activities for students who wish to explore topics further
- Ensure theological content is accurate and biblically grounded within an evangelical Christian tradition
